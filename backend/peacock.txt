Peacocks:

*   Known for vibrant plumage.
*   Male (peafowl) has a striking train of feathers with eye-like markings.
*   Feathers used for courtship.
*   Train can be over six feet long, 60% of body length.
*   Capable of flight, but mainly walk.
*   Native to India and Sri Lanka, introduced elsewhere.
*   Often kept in zoos and parks.
